# Ultrasound-Guided Peripheral IV

## Definition
Ultrasound-guided PIV helps obtain access in difficult vascular access patients.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can reduce repeated blind attempts and speed resuscitation.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use linear probe, identify compressible vein, track needle tip, and confirm catheter position.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Advancing without seeing the needle tip.

## Assessment Question
**Question:** What is the main safety rule in ultrasound-guided PIV placement?

A. It replaces the clinical examination
B. Know where the needle tip is before advancing.
C. It should be interpreted without clinical context
D. It is always definitive even when image quality is poor

**Correct Answer:** B
