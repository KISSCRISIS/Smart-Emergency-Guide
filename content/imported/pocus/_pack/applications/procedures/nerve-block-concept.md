# Ultrasound-Guided Nerve Block Concept

## Definition
POCUS-guided nerve blocks require knowledge of anatomy, needle visualization, local anesthetic safety, and institutional privilege.

## Why it matters in the ED
They can provide targeted analgesia and reduce systemic sedative/opioid needs in selected patients.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Identify nerve, vessels, fascial planes, and needle tip; follow local dosing protocols.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Injecting when anatomy or needle tip is unclear.
