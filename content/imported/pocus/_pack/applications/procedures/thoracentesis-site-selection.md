# Thoracentesis Site Selection

## Definition
POCUS helps identify pleural fluid and safe procedural windows for thoracentesis.

## Why it matters in the ED
It reduces blind needle entry into lung, liver, spleen, or diaphragm.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Mark fluid pocket, diaphragm, lung, and depth; use sterile procedural setup.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Choosing a site from old imaging without bedside confirmation.
