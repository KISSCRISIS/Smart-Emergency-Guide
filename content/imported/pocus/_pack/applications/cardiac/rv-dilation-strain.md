# Right Ventricular Dilation and Strain

## Definition
Focused cardiac POCUS can show RV enlargement or strain patterns that may support obstructive physiology such as massive PE in the right clinical context.

## Why it matters in the ED
RV strain in a hypotensive dyspneic patient can change urgency and differential.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Compare RV size to LV, septal flattening, and overall context.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Diagnosing PE solely from RV dilation without clinical correlation.
