# Left Ventricular Function

## Definition
Focused echo can estimate gross LV systolic function as hyperdynamic, normal, reduced, or severely reduced.

## Why it matters in the ED
LV function helps sort shock and dyspnea: pump failure versus distributive/hypovolemic patterns.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use parasternal long/short, apical, or subcostal views; compare wall thickening and inward movement.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Over-quantifying EF from a limited bedside scan.
