# Cardiac Arrest POCUS

## Definition
POCUS during arrest is a brief pulse-check adjunct to identify reversible causes and cardiac activity without prolonging pauses.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can reveal tamponade, severe RV strain, profound hypovolemia, or organized cardiac motion.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Acquire images only during planned pulse checks and stop if it delays compressions.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Long ultrasound pauses that reduce CPR quality.
