# RUSH Shock Ultrasound

## Definition
RUSH is a structured POCUS approach to shock evaluating pump, tank, and pipes.

## Why it matters in the ED
It helps categorize obstructive, cardiogenic, hypovolemic, and distributive patterns quickly.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Heart, lungs, IVC/abdomen, aorta, and DVT views are combined with resuscitation.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Performing a long protocol instead of a focused exam during instability.
