# Appendicitis Caveats

## Definition
Appendix POCUS can identify a noncompressible enlarged tubular structure in selected patients, especially thin/pediatric patients.

## Why it matters in the ED
A positive scan can help; a negative scan often cannot exclude appendicitis.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use graded compression in RLQ when trained.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Letting a negative limited scan override classic appendicitis concern.

A. It replaces the clinical examination
