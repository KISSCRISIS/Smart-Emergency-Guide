# Long Bone Fracture POCUS

## Definition
MSK POCUS can show cortical disruption in selected long bone fractures.

## Why it matters in the ED
It may help when X-ray is delayed or in procedural reduction guidance, but it is not a universal replacement for radiography.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Scan along cortex in two planes and compare with point tenderness.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Missing injuries away from the scanned focal area.
