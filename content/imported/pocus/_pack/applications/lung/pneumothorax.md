# Pneumothorax POCUS

## Definition
Lung ultrasound can evaluate for pneumothorax by assessing lung sliding, B-lines, lung pulse, and lung point.

## Why it matters in the ED
In unstable patients, rapid recognition can support urgent treatment.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use high-frequency probe over anterior chest in supine patient; compare sides.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Calling absent sliding pneumothorax without considering mainstem intubation, apnea, pleurodesis, or severe COPD.
