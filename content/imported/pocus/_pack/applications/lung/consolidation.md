# Lung Consolidation

## Definition
Consolidated lung may appear tissue-like with dynamic air bronchograms when pneumonia is present.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can help evaluate dyspnea, fever, and focal findings.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Look for pleural irregularity, tissue-like echotexture, and dynamic air bronchograms.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Assuming ultrasound sees all pneumonia, especially deep lesions not touching pleura.
