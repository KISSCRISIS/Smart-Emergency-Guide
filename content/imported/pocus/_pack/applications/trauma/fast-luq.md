# FAST LUQ View

## Definition
The LUQ FAST view evaluates the splenorenal recess, perisplenic space, and left thorax.

## Why it matters in the ED
LUQ fluid can be subtle and more cephalad than beginners expect.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Place probe high and posterior; sweep through spleen, diaphragm, and kidney.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Scanning too low and missing the spleen-diaphragm interface.
