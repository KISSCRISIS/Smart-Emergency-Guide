# FAST RUQ View

## Definition
The RUQ FAST view evaluates for free fluid in Morison’s pouch, perihepatic space, and right thorax.

## Why it matters in the ED
RUQ is often the most sensitive FAST window for intraperitoneal free fluid.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use curvilinear probe in coronal plane; sweep through liver-kidney interface.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Only taking one still image and missing fluid at the diaphragm or inferior pole.
