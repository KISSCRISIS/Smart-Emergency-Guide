# Abscess vs Cellulitis

## Definition
Soft-tissue POCUS can distinguish cobblestoning cellulitis from drainable abscess fluid collection.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can prevent unnecessary incision or identify occult abscess.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use linear probe with light pressure; look for fluid collection, debris, posterior enhancement, and surrounding cobblestoning.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Pressing too hard and compressing a small abscess away.
