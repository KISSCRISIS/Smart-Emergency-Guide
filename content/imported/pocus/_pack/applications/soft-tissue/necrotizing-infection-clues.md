# Soft Tissue Necrotizing Infection Clues

## Definition
Soft-tissue POCUS may reveal gas, fascial fluid, or deep fluid patterns concerning for necrotizing infection, but diagnosis remains clinical and surgical.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can support urgency but should not delay surgical consultation when suspected.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Look for dirty shadowing gas and fascial fluid in context of severe pain/toxicity.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Waiting for ultrasound confirmation before escalating a toxic patient.
