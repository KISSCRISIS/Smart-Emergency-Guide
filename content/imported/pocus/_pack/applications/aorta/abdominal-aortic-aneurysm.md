# Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm

## Definition
Aortic POCUS screens the abdominal aorta for aneurysmal dilation in symptomatic or high-risk patients.

## Why it matters in the ED
Ruptured AAA can present as back/abdominal pain, syncope, or shock.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Scan proximal, mid, distal aorta and bifurcation; measure outer wall to outer wall.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Mistaking IVC for aorta or failing to scan the entire aorta.
