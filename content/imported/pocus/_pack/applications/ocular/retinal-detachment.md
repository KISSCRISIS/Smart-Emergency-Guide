# Retinal Detachment

## Definition
Ocular POCUS can show a mobile hyperechoic membrane tethered to the optic disc in retinal detachment.

## Why it matters in the ED
Abrupt painless vision loss can be time-sensitive.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use high-frequency probe with copious gel and minimal pressure over closed eyelid; avoid if open globe suspected.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Scanning an eye with suspected globe rupture.
