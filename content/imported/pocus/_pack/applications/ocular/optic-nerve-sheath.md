# Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter

## Definition
ONSD ultrasound may support suspicion of elevated intracranial pressure but is not a standalone diagnostic test.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can add information when CT/clinical findings suggest raised ICP.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Measure behind globe according to local training standards.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Using ONSD alone to rule in/out elevated ICP.
