# Vitreous Hemorrhage vs Detachment

## Definition
Ocular POCUS may show mobile echoes in vitreous hemorrhage or membranes in vitreous/retinal detachment patterns.

## Why it matters in the ED
It helps triage visual complaints but ophthalmology correlation is important.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Dynamic scanning with gentle eye movement can help distinguish patterns.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Overcalling vitreous changes as retinal detachment.
