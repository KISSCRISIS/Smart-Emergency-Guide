# Bladder Volume and Retention

## Definition
Bladder POCUS estimates bladder distention and can support urinary retention diagnosis.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can prevent unnecessary catheterization or confirm obstruction in altered/elderly patients.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Scan suprapubic transverse and sagittal views.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Mistaking pelvic cystic structures for bladder without landmarks.
