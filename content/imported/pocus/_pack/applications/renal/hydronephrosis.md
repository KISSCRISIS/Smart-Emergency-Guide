# Hydronephrosis

## Definition
Renal POCUS can identify hydronephrosis as a sign of urinary obstruction.

## Why it matters in the ED
It supports renal colic evaluation and identifies high-risk obstruction patterns with infection, solitary kidney, or renal failure.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Scan both kidneys in long and short axes.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Assuming no hydronephrosis excludes early ureteral stone.
