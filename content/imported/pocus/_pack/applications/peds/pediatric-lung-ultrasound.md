# Pediatric Lung Ultrasound

## Definition
Lung POCUS can help evaluate pediatric pneumonia, effusion, and pneumothorax patterns when lesions reach the pleura.

## Why it matters in the ED
It avoids radiation and can be repeated, but deep lesions may be missed.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use linear or curvilinear probe depending on body size and depth.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Assuming a normal lung scan excludes all pneumonia.

## Assessment Question
**Question:** What limits pediatric lung POCUS for pneumonia?

A. It replaces the clinical examination
B. Lesions not reaching the pleural surface may be missed.
C. It should be interpreted without clinical context
D. It is always definitive even when image quality is poor

**Correct Answer:** B
