# DVT Popliteal Compression

## Definition
Popliteal compression evaluates the popliteal vein behind the knee as part of proximal DVT scanning.

## Why it matters in the ED
Missing this region can miss clinically important proximal DVT.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Scan in popliteal fossa and compress vein fully.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Stopping after only the groin.
