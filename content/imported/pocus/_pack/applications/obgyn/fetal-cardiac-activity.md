# Fetal Cardiac Activity Basics

## Definition
POCUS can document fetal cardiac activity when within operator scope and appropriate gestational age.

## Why it matters in the ED
It can guide urgency and counseling but does not replace full obstetric assessment.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use M-mode rather than pulsed Doppler when documenting early fetal heart activity.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Using Doppler unnecessarily in early pregnancy teaching scans.
