# Ectopic Pregnancy Danger

## Definition
POCUS may identify findings concerning for ectopic pregnancy, such as extrauterine gestational structures or free fluid in a pregnant patient.

## Why it matters in the ED
Ruptured ectopic can cause hemorrhagic shock.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Assess uterus, adnexa when trained, and free fluid; integrate vitals and symptoms.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Waiting for definitive adnexal findings while the patient is unstable.

A. It replaces the clinical examination
