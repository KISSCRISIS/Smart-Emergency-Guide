# Procedural Sterile Setup

## Definition
Ultrasound-guided invasive procedures need sterile probe cover, sterile gel, and a controlled field when entering sterile spaces.

## Why it matters in the ED
A clean diagnostic scan is not the same as a sterile procedural scan.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Prepare the field before touching the sterile needle.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Starting a sterile procedure then trying to fix the probe cover mid-procedure.
