# Needle Visualization

## Definition
Ultrasound-guided procedures require deliberate needle tracking, preferably keeping the needle tip in view.

## Why it matters in the ED
The danger is not the needle; it is losing the tip and advancing blindly.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use in-plane or out-of-plane technique with clear tip awareness.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Seeing the shaft and assuming the tip is safe.

## Assessment Question
**Question:** What is the safest principle during ultrasound-guided needle procedures?

A. It replaces the clinical examination
B. Keep the needle tip identified before advancing.
C. It should be interpreted without clinical context
D. It is always definitive even when image quality is poor

**Correct Answer:** B
