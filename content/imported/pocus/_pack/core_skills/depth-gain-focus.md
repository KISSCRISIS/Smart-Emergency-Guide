# Depth, Gain, and Focus

## Definition
Depth determines how far you see, gain determines brightness, and focus optimizes resolution at a selected depth.

## Why it matters in the ED
Poor knobology can mimic pathology or hide it.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Set depth first, then gain, then focus.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Turning gain up until fluid disappears or tissue becomes falsely bright.
