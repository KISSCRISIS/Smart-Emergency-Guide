# Probe Cleaning and Infection Control

## Definition
Probe cleaning depends on contact level: intact skin, mucous membrane, sterile field, or contaminated fluid exposure.

## Why it matters in the ED
Ultrasound can transmit infection if probes and gel are handled poorly.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Follow institutional cleaning and disinfection policies.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Using the same probe/gel on multiple patients without appropriate cleaning.
