# Linear Probe

## Definition
A high-frequency probe for superficial structures such as vessels, soft tissue, ocular scanning, nerve blocks, and pneumothorax pleural line.

## Why it matters in the ED
High resolution but limited depth.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use for procedures and superficial diagnostic targets.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Looking too deep and losing resolution advantage.
