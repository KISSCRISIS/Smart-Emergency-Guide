# Curvilinear Probe

## Definition
A low-frequency probe with deeper penetration, useful for abdominal, pelvic, aortic, renal, and many FAST views.

## Why it matters in the ED
Large field and depth make it ideal for deep abdominal targets.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Use gel generously; begin with wide survey then refine.

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Using it for superficial tiny structures when linear probe would be clearer.
