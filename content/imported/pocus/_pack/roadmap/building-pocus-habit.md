# Building a POCUS Habit in SEG

## Definition
The SEG POCUS habit is: define the question, choose the probe, get landmarks, identify danger pattern, state the limitation, and reassess after intervention.

## Why it matters in the ED
Repetition creates pattern recognition without overconfidence.

## Probe / Patient / Acquisition
Every topic page should end with a focused question: What am I looking for, and what can kill the patient now?

## Key Findings to Look For
- Start with the focused clinical question.
- Confirm the relevant anatomy before interpreting pathology.
- Compare sides or views when appropriate.
- Save representative images or clips for QA when the scan is used clinically.

## Common ED Trap
Scanning without a clinical question.
