# SEG POCUS Essentials Content Pack

Status: SITE_CONTENT_BASELINE_V1
Created: 2026-07-03

## Purpose
This pack prepares standalone educational content pages for the Smart Emergency Guide (SEG) Point-of-Care Ultrasound section.

## Source Policy
- Primary board-style source: Rosen’s Emergency Medicine 10e Q&A, especially the Emergency Ultrasound chapter and related chapter references.
- Secondary board-style support: Tintinalli’s Emergency Medicine Examination and Board Review 2023, Chapter 25 Point-of-Care Ultrasound.
- Current guideline/QA support: ACEP Emergency Ultrasound Guidelines and AIUM practice parameters.

The content is original SEG educational writing and does not copy source questions or explanations verbatim.

## Structure
- `roadmap/`: beginner concepts and safe POCUS habits.
- `core_skills/`: probe, orientation, knobology, artifacts, procedural safety.
- `applications/`: clinical application pages.
- `practice/`: MCQs, mini cases, rapid prompts.
- `metadata/`: JSON/CSV index, route skeleton, image briefs.
- `references.md`, `source_mapping.md`, `safety_audit.md`, `qa_checklist.md`.

## SEG Rules Preserved
- Standalone short pages.
- English academic/board-style content.
- Arabic simplification and reasoning.
- No one-page stacking.
- No final local credentialing or privileging claims.
- Images are briefs only; final visuals are deferred to the website phase.
