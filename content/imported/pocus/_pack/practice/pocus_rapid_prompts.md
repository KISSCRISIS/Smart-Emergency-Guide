# POCUS Rapid Prompts

Use these as app cards or drills.

1. What is the focused question?
2. Which probe and preset?
3. What landmark proves you are in the right place?
4. What finding changes management now?
5. What can kill the patient now?
6. What is the most common false-positive?
7. What is the most common false-negative?
8. What should be documented?
9. When should this scan not delay intervention?
10. When does this patient need formal imaging or consultant escalation?
