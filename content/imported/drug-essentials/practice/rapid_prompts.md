# Drug Essentials Rapid Prompts

Use these as flashcard prompts inside SEG.

- What problem does this drug solve?
- What physiologic failure is present?
- What is the first monitoring step before or immediately after administration?
- What adverse effect would make you stop or change course?
- Is the patient adult, pediatric, pregnant, renal failure, hepatic failure, or anticoagulated?
- Does this drug require ECG monitoring?
- Does this drug depress breathing?
- Could this drug worsen hypotension?
- Could this drug cause bleeding?
- Could this drug require antidote or reversal?
- Is final dosing verified by local protocol/formulary?
