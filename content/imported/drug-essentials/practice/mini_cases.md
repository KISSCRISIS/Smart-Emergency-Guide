# Drug Essentials Mini Cases

## How to Use
Each case asks three SEG questions:
1. What problem is the drug trying to solve?
2. What can go wrong after giving it?
3. What must be verified before final dosing?

## Case: Norepinephrine
**Scenario:** A hypotensive septic patient remains poorly perfused after initial resuscitation.

**Best drug-thinking target:** Norepinephrine

**Problem being treated:** vasodilatory shock support

**What can kill the patient now?**
Unrecognized deterioration, wrong physiology, delayed definitive care, or drug-related adverse effect.

**Monitoring focus:** perfusion endpoints, rhythm, extravasation, source control

**Arabic simplification:** لا تختار الدواء لأنه مشهور؛ اختره لأن المشكلة التي أمامك تحتاج آليته تحديدًا، ثم راقب الخطر المتوقع.
