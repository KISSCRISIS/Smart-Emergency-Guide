# Drug Essentials MCQ Bank

> These are original SEG-style educational questions. They are not copied from any board-review book. Numeric dosing is intentionally excluded until the verified dosing layer is built.

## Question 1
A learner is reviewing **Epinephrine** before an ED shift. What is the safest general approach?

A. Memorize a single universal dose and use it for all patients.
B. Link the drug to a specific clinical indication, anticipate monitoring needs, and verify final dosing through local protocol/formulary.
C. Use the drug whenever the diagnosis is uncertain.
D. Avoid documenting reassessment after administration.

**Answer: B**

**Arabic explanation:** الدواء في الطوارئ ليس اسمًا تحفظه فقط؛ هو قرار مرتبط بحالة المريض، ومراقبة، وجرعة يجب التحقق منها قبل الاستخدام السريري.
