# Source Mapping

## Why Tintinalli Was Used
Tintinalli’s Emergency Medicine Examination and Board Review is useful for:
- identifying high-yield emergency medications;
- board-style clinical contexts;
- common exam traps;
- linking drugs to ED syndromes.

## Why Tintinalli Was Not Used Alone for Final Doses
Medication dosing changes with:
- patient age and weight;
- pregnancy;
- renal/hepatic function;
- route and concentration;
- hospital formulary;
- national/local protocols;
- updated labeling and guidelines.

Therefore, each drug page includes a dose placeholder and must undergo final verification before publication.
