# Left Bundle Branch Block

## Core Definition
LBBB is delayed left ventricular activation due to left bundle conduction delay.

## ECG Recognition Clues
QRS ≥120 ms; broad/notched R waves in I, aVL, V5-V6; deep S or QS in V1; discordant secondary ST-T changes.

## Why It Matters in the ED
New or presumed new LBBB with ischemic symptoms is high-risk; use appropriate criteria and clinical escalation.

## Common Trap
Do not interpret ST segments in LBBB using ordinary STEMI rules without considering discordance and modified criteria.

## Assessment Question
Broad notched R waves in lateral leads with deep S in V1 suggests what?
A) LBBB
B) RBBB
C) AF
D) Hyperkalemia only

**Answer:** A) LBBB

**Arabic Rationale:** Lateral broad R + V1 deep S = LBBB pattern.
