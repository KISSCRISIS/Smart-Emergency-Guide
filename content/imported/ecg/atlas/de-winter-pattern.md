# De Winter Pattern

## Core Definition
De Winter pattern is an anterior OMI equivalent associated with proximal LAD occlusion.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Upsloping ST depression at the J point in precordial leads with tall symmetric T waves; often slight ST elevation in aVR.

## Why It Matters in the ED
It may represent acute coronary occlusion without classic ST elevation.

## Common Trap
Do not wait for classic STEMI criteria if the clinical story and pattern suggest occlusion.

## Assessment Question
Upsloping ST depression with tall symmetric T waves in V2-V6 during chest pain suggests what?
A) De Winter OMI equivalent
B) Normal variant always
C) Complete heart block
D) Hypokalemia

**Answer:** A) De Winter OMI equivalent

**Arabic Rationale:** هذا نمط LAD occlusion equivalent، وليس مجرد ST depression عادي.
