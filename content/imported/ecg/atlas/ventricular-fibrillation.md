# Ventricular Fibrillation

## Core Definition
Ventricular fibrillation is disorganized ventricular electrical activity without effective ventricular contraction.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Chaotic irregular waveform, no organized QRS complexes, no measurable rhythm.

## Why It Matters in the ED
It is a shockable cardiac arrest rhythm and requires immediate resuscitation response.

## Common Trap
Fine VF may look like asystole; verify connections but do not delay resuscitation in a pulseless patient.

## Assessment Question
No pulse plus chaotic ECG with no QRS complexes indicates what?
A) VF
B) AF
C) Sinus rhythm
D) Mobitz I

**Answer:** A) VF
