# Second-Degree AV Block: Mobitz I

## Core Definition
Mobitz I, or Wenckebach, is progressive PR prolongation until a QRS complex is dropped.

## ECG Recognition Clues
PR interval lengthens beat by beat, then a P wave is not followed by QRS; cycle repeats.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Often AV nodal and may be transient, but symptoms and clinical context matter.

## Common Trap
Do not confuse grouped beating with premature beats without marking P waves.

## Assessment Question
Progressive PR lengthening followed by dropped QRS suggests what?
A) Mobitz I
B) Mobitz II
C) VF
D) Brugada

**Answer:** A) Mobitz I

**Arabic Rationale:** Wenckebach = longer, longer, longer, drop.
