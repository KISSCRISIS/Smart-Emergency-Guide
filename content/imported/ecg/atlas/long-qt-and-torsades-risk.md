# Long QT and Torsades Risk

## Core Definition
Long QT is delayed ventricular repolarization that increases risk of polymorphic VT, including torsades de pointes.

## ECG Recognition Clues
QTc prolonged, abnormal T/U morphology, possible pause-dependent polymorphic VT twisting around baseline.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Can cause syncope, seizures or sudden death; may be congenital or acquired from drugs, low K/Mg/Ca, CNS injury or ischemia.

## Common Trap
Do not ignore a prolonged QT in a patient with syncope.
