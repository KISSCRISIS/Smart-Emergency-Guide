# Calcium and QT Changes

## Core Definition
Calcium abnormalities primarily affect the ST segment and QT interval.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Hypocalcemia prolongs QT; hypercalcemia shortens QT. QRS duration is usually not the main feature.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Marked QT prolongation increases risk of ventricular arrhythmia, while very short QT may suggest hypercalcemia in context.

## Common Trap
Always correct QT interpretation for heart rate and avoid relying only on automated measurement.
