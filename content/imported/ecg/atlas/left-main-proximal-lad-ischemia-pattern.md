# Left Main / Proximal LAD Ischemia Pattern

## Core Definition
Diffuse ST depression with ST elevation in aVR can suggest global subendocardial ischemia, including left main, proximal LAD or severe multivessel disease in the right context.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Widespread horizontal/downsloping ST depression, often maximal in lateral/inferior leads, with ST elevation in aVR and sometimes V1.

## Why It Matters in the ED
This is a high-risk ACS/shock pattern and requires urgent escalation.

## Common Trap
It is not specific for left main alone; severe demand ischemia can also cause diffuse ST depression. Interpret with clinical context.
