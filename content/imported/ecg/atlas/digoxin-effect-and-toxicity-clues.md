# Digoxin Effect and Toxicity Clues

## Core Definition
Digoxin effect can cause characteristic scooped ST depression, while toxicity may cause many arrhythmias.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Scooped downsloping ST depression, shortened QT; toxicity may show PVCs, AV block, atrial tachycardia with block, bidirectional VT.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Important because symptoms can be vague and arrhythmias dangerous, especially with renal dysfunction or electrolyte abnormalities.

## Common Trap
Do not call scooped ST depression ischemia automatically without medication context.

## Assessment Question
Scooped ST depression in a patient taking digoxin is called what?
A) Digoxin effect
B) VF
C) Brugada
D) Posterior MI

**Answer:** A) Digoxin effect
