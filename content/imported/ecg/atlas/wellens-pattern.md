# Wellens Pattern

## Core Definition
Wellens pattern is a high-risk T-wave pattern associated with critical LAD disease after recent chest pain.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Biphasic or deep symmetric T-wave inversion in V2-V3, minimal ST elevation, no pathologic Q waves, pain often resolved.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Predicts risk of large anterior MI if missed.

## Common Trap
Avoid stress testing in suspected Wellens until specialist assessment.

## Assessment Question
Deep T inversions in V2-V3 after resolved chest pain suggest what?
A) Wellens
B) Flutter
C) Asystole
D) Normal sinus rhythm

**Answer:** A) Wellens
