# Wolff-Parkinson-White Pattern

## Core Definition
WPW pattern reflects ventricular preexcitation through an accessory pathway.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Short PR interval <120 ms, slurred delta wave at QRS onset, widened QRS and secondary ST-T changes.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Important because AF with preexcitation can conduct rapidly to ventricles and become unstable.

## Common Trap
Do not treat preexcited AF as ordinary narrow-complex AF; identify the wide/variable preexcited pattern.

## Assessment Question
Short PR plus delta wave suggests what?
A) WPW pattern
B) First-degree AV block
C) Asystole
D) Wellens

**Answer:** A) WPW pattern
