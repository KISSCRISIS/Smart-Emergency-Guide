# Brugada Pattern

## Core Definition
Brugada type 1 pattern is coved ST elevation with T inversion in right precordial leads and may signal inherited arrhythmia risk.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Coved ST elevation in V1-V2, often with RBBB-like appearance, followed by negative T wave; may be fever-provoked.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Important in syncope or family history of sudden death.

## Common Trap
High V1/V2 placement can alter morphology; repeat ECG with proper placement and senior review.
