# Inferior STEMI and Right Ventricular Involvement

## Core Definition
Inferior STEMI affects the inferior wall, often supplied by RCA or LCx depending on dominance; RCA occlusion may involve the right ventricle.

## ECG Recognition Clues
ST elevation in II, III, aVF; reciprocal ST depression in I/aVL. ST elevation in III > II may suggest RCA. Right-sided leads can reveal RV involvement.

## Why It Matters in the ED
RV involvement can make the patient preload-sensitive and prone to hypotension.

## Common Trap
Do not miss complete heart block in inferior MI; check rhythm and AV conduction carefully.
