# Left Anterior Fascicular Block

## Core Definition
LAFB is conduction delay/block in the left anterior fascicle, causing left axis deviation.

## ECG Recognition Clues
Left axis deviation, qR in I/aVL, rS in inferior leads, QRS usually not very wide unless combined with other block.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Common cause of LAD; may be chronic or reflect conduction system disease.

## Common Trap
Do not diagnose inferior MI from inferior rS pattern without assessing Q waves and context.

## Assessment Question
Lead I positive and inferior leads with rS pattern most suggests what?
A) LAFB
B) VF
C) WPW
D) Brugada

**Answer:** A) LAFB
