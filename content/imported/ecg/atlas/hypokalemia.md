# Hypokalemia

## Core Definition
Hypokalemia delays repolarization and may produce characteristic ST-T-U changes.

## ECG Recognition Clues
ST depression, flattened T waves, prominent U waves, apparent long QU interval and ventricular ectopy risk.

## Why It Matters in the ED
Severe hypokalemia increases arrhythmia risk, especially with digoxin, ischemia or QT-prolonging conditions.

## Common Trap
Do not mistake U waves for T waves when measuring QT; this may distort risk estimation.
