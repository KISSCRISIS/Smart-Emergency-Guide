# Right Bundle Branch Block

## Core Definition
RBBB is delayed right ventricular activation due to right bundle conduction delay.

## ECG Recognition Clues
QRS ≥120 ms in complete RBBB; rSR' or M-shaped pattern in V1-V2; broad terminal S waves in I and V6; secondary ST-T changes may occur.

## Why It Matters in the ED
May be chronic or associated with PE, ischemia, myocarditis or structural disease depending on context.

## Common Trap
High V1/V2 placement can mimic incomplete RBBB. Check lead placement.

## Assessment Question
rSR' in V1 with broad terminal S in I/V6 suggests what?
A) RBBB
B) LBBB
C) VF
D) Wellens

**Answer:** A) RBBB

**Arabic Rationale:** V1 هو كاميرا البطين الأيمن؛ التأخر يظهر هناك كـ R' أخيرة.
