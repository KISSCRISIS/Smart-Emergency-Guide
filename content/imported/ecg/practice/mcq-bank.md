# ECG Practice MCQ Bank

Academic English questions with Arabic teaching rationale.

## MCQ 1
At 25 mm/s, one small ECG box equals:

A) 0.01 s

B) 0.04 s

C) 0.10 s

D) 0.20 s

**Answer:** B

**Arabic Rationale:** كل مربع صغير أفقي = 0.04 ثانية.

## MCQ 2
A regular rhythm has 5 large boxes between R waves. The rate is approximately:

A) 50 bpm

B) 60 bpm

C) 75 bpm

D) 100 bpm

**Answer:** B

**Arabic Rationale:** 300 ÷ 5 = 60.

## MCQ 3
Irregularly irregular rhythm with no discrete P waves is most consistent with:

A) Atrial fibrillation

B) Complete heart block

C) First-degree AV block

D) Asystole

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** AF = لا P واضحة + R-R عشوائي.

## MCQ 4
Progressive PR prolongation followed by a dropped QRS is:

A) Mobitz I

B) Mobitz II

C) WPW

D) RBBB

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** Wenckebach = يطول ثم يسقط.

## MCQ 5
Constant PR intervals with sudden dropped QRS complexes suggest:

A) Mobitz I

B) Mobitz II

C) Normal sinus rhythm

D) Atrial flutter

**Answer:** B

**Arabic Rationale:** السقوط المفاجئ دون إطالة تدريجية أخطر ويدعم Mobitz II.

## MCQ 6
Complete AV dissociation is the key feature of:

A) Complete heart block

B) First-degree AV block

C) WPW

D) Sinus tachycardia

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** الأذين والبطين مستقلان تمامًا.

## MCQ 7
Short PR interval with delta wave suggests:

A) WPW

B) Wellens

C) Pericarditis

D) Hyperkalemia

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** طريق مختصر accessory pathway.

## MCQ 8
Tall tented T waves with widening QRS suggests:

A) Hyperkalemia

B) Hypokalemia

C) Atrial flutter

D) LAFB

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** الخيمة المدببة واتساع QRS علامة بوتاسيوم خطير.

## MCQ 9
Deep symmetric T-wave inversion in V2-V3 after resolved chest pain suggests:

A) Wellens syndrome

B) Benign sinus arrhythmia

C) VF

D) Lead reversal only

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** تحذير LAD عالي الخطورة.

## MCQ 10
Coved ST elevation with T inversion in V1-V2 suggests:

A) Brugada type 1 pattern

B) Inferior STEMI

C) Atrial flutter

D) Hypocalcemia

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** V1/V2 coved pattern هو المفتاح.

## MCQ 11
ST elevation in II, III, aVF localizes to:

A) Inferior wall

B) Anterior wall

C) High lateral wall

D) Posterior wall only

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** II/III/aVF تنظر للأسفل.

## MCQ 12
ST elevation in V2-V4 most strongly suggests injury in:

A) Anterior/septal territory

B) Inferior wall

C) Right atrium

D) Lead reversal

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** V1-V4 أمامية/حاجزية.

## MCQ 13
Horizontal ST depression in V1-V3 with tall R waves during chest pain suggests:

A) Posterior MI

B) First-degree AV block

C) Sinus bradycardia

D) Atrial flutter

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** الخلف يظهر كمرآة في الأمام.

## MCQ 14
Diffuse concave ST elevation with PR depression suggests:

A) Pericarditis

B) WPW

C) TCA toxicity

D) Mobitz II

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** التهاب التامور ينتشر ولا يتبع شريانًا واحدًا.

## MCQ 15
Regular wide-complex tachycardia in an older patient with prior MI should be assumed to be:

A) VT until proven otherwise

B) Normal sinus rhythm

C) Always atrial flutter

D) Artifact only

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** قاعدة أمان في الطوارئ.

## MCQ 16
Chaotic ECG without organized QRS in a pulseless patient is:

A) VF

B) AF

C) Sinus tachycardia

D) First-degree block

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** VF = لا QRS منظم ولا ضخ.

## MCQ 17
Flatline in a pulseless patient should prompt CPR and confirmation of:

A) Lead connection/gain and another lead

B) STEMI territory

C) QTc only

D) Delta wave

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** تأكد أنه ليس سلكًا مفصولًا، لكن لا تؤخر الإنعاش.

## MCQ 18
Diffuse ST depression with ST elevation in aVR during ischemic symptoms suggests:

A) High-risk global ischemia

B) Normal ECG

C) WPW only

D) Hypokalemia only

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** نمط واسع وخطير يحتاج تصعيد.

## MCQ 19
Prominent U waves with flattened T waves suggest:

A) Hypokalemia

B) Hyperkalemia

C) Brugada

D) VF

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** U wave واضحة مع T مسطحة = فكر K منخفض.

## MCQ 20
Markedly prolonged QTc is associated with risk of:

A) Polymorphic VT/torsades

B) Only first-degree AV block

C) Only axis deviation

D) No arrhythmia risk

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** QT الطويل يفتح باب torsades.

## MCQ 21
rSR' in V1 with broad terminal S in I/V6 suggests:

A) RBBB

B) LBBB

C) Wellens

D) Asystole

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** تأخر البطين الأيمن يظهر كـ R' في V1.

## MCQ 22
Broad notched R waves in I/V6 and deep S in V1 suggest:

A) LBBB

B) RBBB

C) AF

D) VF

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** تأخر البطين الأيسر يعطي LBBB morphology.

## MCQ 23
Atrial flutter with 2:1 conduction often has ventricular rate near:

A) 150 bpm

B) 40 bpm

C) 300 bpm

D) 20 bpm

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** كل موجتين أذينيتين تعطي ضربة بطينية تقريبًا.

## MCQ 24
Pacing spike not followed by QRS suggests:

A) Failure to capture

B) Normal capture

C) Wellens

D) Brugada

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** شرارة دون استجابة.

## MCQ 25
A wide QRS overdose ECG with terminal R in aVR suggests:

A) Sodium-channel blocker/TCA toxicity

B) Normal ECG

C) Sinus arrhythmia

D) Pericarditis

**Answer:** A

**Arabic Rationale:** QRS الواسع + aVR في التسممات مهم جدًا.
