# Rapid ECG Prompts: First Thing / Killer Question

## Prompt 1
**First thing you notice:** Irregularly irregular + no P

**Pattern:** Atrial fibrillation

**What can kill the patient now?** rapid ventricular response / embolic risk / instability depending on patient


## Prompt 2
**First thing you notice:** No pulse + chaotic no QRS

**Pattern:** VF

**What can kill the patient now?** cardiac arrest


## Prompt 3
**First thing you notice:** No pulse + regular wide tachy

**Pattern:** Pulseless VT

**What can kill the patient now?** cardiac arrest


## Prompt 4
**First thing you notice:** Flatline confirmed

**Pattern:** Asystole

**What can kill the patient now?** cardiac arrest


## Prompt 5
**First thing you notice:** V2-V4 ST elevation

**Pattern:** Anterior OMI/STEMI

**What can kill the patient now?** expanding LAD infarction


## Prompt 6
**First thing you notice:** II III aVF ST elevation

**Pattern:** Inferior STEMI

**What can kill the patient now?** RV infarct, AV block, shock


## Prompt 7
**First thing you notice:** V1-V3 ST depression + tall R

**Pattern:** Posterior MI

**What can kill the patient now?** missed occlusion


## Prompt 8
**First thing you notice:** V2-V3 deep/biphasic T after pain

**Pattern:** Wellens

**What can kill the patient now?** large anterior MI if missed


## Prompt 9
**First thing you notice:** Tented T + wide QRS

**Pattern:** Hyperkalemia

**What can kill the patient now?** VF/asystole


## Prompt 10
**First thing you notice:** Coved ST V1-V2

**Pattern:** Brugada type 1

**What can kill the patient now?** ventricular arrhythmia/sudden death risk


## Prompt 11
**First thing you notice:** Short PR + delta wave

**Pattern:** WPW

**What can kill the patient now?** preexcited AF danger


## Prompt 12
**First thing you notice:** Long QTc

**Pattern:** Long QT

**What can kill the patient now?** torsades/polymorphic VT


## Prompt 13
**First thing you notice:** Wide QRS overdose + aVR R

**Pattern:** TCA/sodium channel blockade

**What can kill the patient now?** seizure/VT/shock


## Prompt 14
**First thing you notice:** P and QRS independent

**Pattern:** Complete heart block

**What can kill the patient now?** brady shock/arrest


## Prompt 15
**First thing you notice:** Regular narrow 150 + sawtooth

**Pattern:** Atrial flutter 2:1

**What can kill the patient now?** rapid rate decompensation
