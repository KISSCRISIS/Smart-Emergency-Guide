# Source Mapping — SEG Critical Care Essentials

## Primary project source
- Tintinalli’s Emergency Medicine Examination and Board Review, 2023.
- Used for board-style mapping, emergency medicine framing, and topic selection.
- Not copied verbatim.

## Supporting guideline domains for final audit
- AHA CPR/ECC for cardiac arrest, peri-arrest, and post-arrest topics.
- Surviving Sepsis Campaign for sepsis and septic shock.
- ADA Standards/consensus material for hyperglycemic emergencies.
- GOLD for COPD exacerbation framework.
- Local hospital policies for airway, ventilator settings, vasopressors, blood products, ICU admission, and transfer.

## Safety policy
No numeric drug doses, infusion rates, rigid timing promises, fixed ventilator settings, or absolute disposition thresholds are final in this baseline.
