Medication doses, ventilator settings, procedure timing, transfusion ratios, and disposition rules must be verified against local policy and current specialty guidelines before publication or clinical use.



## Question 1: Undifferentiated Shock

A patient presents with hypotension or narrow pulse pressure. Which SEG reasoning step is most appropriate?

A. Ignore physiology until the final diagnosis is confirmed.
B. Identify immediate threats to oxygenation, ventilation, perfusion, and neurologic safety.
C. Treat the monitor value only and avoid reassessment.
D. Transfer without communicating unresolved risks.

**Correct Answer:** B

**Arabic Explanation:** في الحالات الحرجة لا نعالج الاسم فقط، بل نعالج الخطر الذي قد يقتل الآن: نقص الأكسجين، فشل التهوية، الصدمة، اضطراب النظم، أو تدهور الوعي.
