# Auto-PEEP and Breath Stacking


## Definition
Auto-PEEP occurs when the patient does not fully exhale before the next breath, trapping gas and increasing intrathoracic pressure.

## Why It Matters in the ED
It can cause hypotension, barotrauma, and arrest, especially in asthma/COPD.

## First 60-Second Impression
Look at mental status, work of breathing, oxygen saturation trend, capnography if available, hemodynamics, and whether the patient can protect and clear the airway.

## Key ED Clues
- expiratory flow not returning to baseline
- high peak pressures
- worsening hypotension
- obstructive disease
- rapid ventilator rate

## Rosen-Based Educational Draft
- Rosen-based concept: obstructive lung disease patients are particularly vulnerable to decompensation when expiratory time is inadequate.

**Final status:** Requires final local protocol/formulary verification before bedside or public clinical use.

## Conceptual First ED Actions
1. Reduce breath stacking by increasing expiratory time according to protocol.
2. Treat bronchospasm.
3. Consider brief disconnect in peri-arrest dynamic hyperinflation with expert/local protocol guidance.

## Common Trap
Treating the monitor number without asking whether the patient is oxygenating, ventilating, and perfusing.
