# Mini Cases

## Case 1
**Clinical stem:** Agitated hypoxemic pneumonia patient ripping off the mask.

**What can kill the patient now?** Hypoxemia, hypercapnia, shock, failed oxygenation, or delayed escalation.

**Best learning move:** Consider DSI-style preparation: dissociate only with full airway readiness, improve oxygen reserve, then proceed according to local RSI pathway.

**Arabic simplification:** لا تبحث عن اسم التشخيص فقط؛ ابحث عن الخطر الفوري: هواء؟ أكسجين؟ CO₂؟ ضغط؟ ثم تصرف.
