## Final verification sources required before publication
- Local ED / ICU / anesthesia airway policy.
- Local RSI medication formulary and dosing policy.
- Local mechanical ventilation protocol and ventilator model settings.
- Difficult Airway Society guidelines for difficult/failed intubation.
- AHA CPR/ECC guidance for advanced airway during resuscitation and capnography.
- ARDSNet/lung-protective ventilation evidence for ARDS concepts.
- ACEP procedural sedation policy / local sedation governance.
