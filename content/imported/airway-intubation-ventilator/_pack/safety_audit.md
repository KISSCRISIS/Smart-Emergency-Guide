# Safety Audit

## Safety status
This pack intentionally includes some Rosen-based educational draft doses and ventilator setting drafts because the user approved this approach for Airway/Intubation/Ventilator content.

## Mandatory labels
Every page includes a SEG Safety Boundary stating that the material is educational and requires local protocol verification before bedside or public use.

## High-risk items requiring final audit
- RSI induction and paralytic doses.
- Pediatric airway rescue recommendations.
- Failed airway algorithm wording.
- CICO/front-of-neck access flow.
- Ventilator initial settings.
- ARDS/lung-protective thresholds.
- Asthma/COPD permissive hypercapnia and disconnect/rescue logic.
- Post-intubation sedation/analgesia choices.
- Procedural sedation dosing and fasting policy.

## What is intentionally not finalized
- No local drug concentration, infusion setup, or formulary-specific instructions.
- No hospital-specific airway team activation criteria.
- No device-specific ventilator button instructions.
- No absolute disposition rules.

## Required before public clinical release
1. Emergency medicine consultant review.
2. Local anesthesia/airway lead review for failed airway and CICO pages.
3. ICU/respiratory therapy review for ventilator pages.
4. Pharmacy review for RSI and sedation drug pages.
5. Pediatric EM review for pediatric airway content.
