# SEG Airway, Intubation & Ventilator Essentials Content Pack

Generated: 2026-07-03

This is the sixth SEG content baseline after ECG, Toxicology, Drug Essentials, Emergency Cases, and Critical Care Essentials.

## Purpose
Create a standalone, site-ready content baseline for emergency airway, RSI, post-intubation care, and ventilator thinking for interns, general practitioners, and emergency medicine residents.

## Primary Source Policy
- Primary board-style source: **Rosen's Emergency Medicine 10e Q&A**.
- Secondary board-style support when relevant: **Tintinalli's Emergency Medicine Examination and Board Review 2023**.
- Rosen-based dose/setting/algorithm drafts are allowed in this pack, but every such item is labeled as educational draft content requiring final local protocol verification.

## Structure
- `content/roadmap/` — beginner roadmap pages
- `content/airway/` — airway assessment and rescue pages
- `content/intubation/` — RSI, difficult airway, and medication draft pages
- `content/ventilator/` — ventilator concepts, settings drafts, alarms, and troubleshooting pages
- `practice/` — MCQ bank, mini cases, rapid prompts
- `data/` — JSON and CSV index
- `routes/` — TypeScript route skeleton
- `assets/image_briefs.csv` — visual requirements
- `references.md`
- `source_mapping.md`
- `safety_audit.md`
- `qa_checklist.md`

## SEG Rules Used
- Short independent pages, not one-page stacking.
- Official academic English for scientific content.
- Arabic simplification for analogies and reasoning.
- Board-style assessment question on each page.
- Rosen-based educational draft doses/settings included only where pedagogically useful.
- Final local protocol verification is mandatory before public clinical use.
- Final visuals and site styling are deferred to the website phase.
