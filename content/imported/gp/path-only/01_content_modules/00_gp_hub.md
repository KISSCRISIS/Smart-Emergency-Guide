## Path Purpose

This pathway is built for the doctor who sees the patient before the emergency department.

The GP must be excellent at:

- recognizing danger early
- avoiding false reassurance
- knowing when clinic care is not enough
- escalating to ED/ambulance/specialist care
- writing clear referrals
- documenting safety-netting
