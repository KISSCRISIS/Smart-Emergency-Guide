# GP Safety-Netting

## Purpose

Teach safe return advice when ED referral is not immediately required.

## Safety-netting must include

- what diagnosis is suspected
- what uncertainty remains
- what warning signs require urgent care
- when to recheck
- what to do if symptoms worsen
- who to contact
- confirmation that the patient understood

## Warning signs needing urgent reassessment

- worsening pain
- new chest pain
- shortness of breath
- syncope
- confusion
- weakness/numbness/speech changes
- persistent vomiting
- dehydration
- fever with deterioration
- non-blanching rash
- bleeding
- reduced urine output
- worsening headache
- severe abdominal pain
- any new concerning symptom
