# GP Path Medical Review Risk Register

| Module | Risk Level | Main Risk | Mitigation | Review Needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Chest Pain | Critical | missed ACS/dissection/PE | red flags + urgent referral | EM/Cardiology |
| Dyspnea | Critical | missed respiratory failure/PE/anaphylaxis | red flags + escalation | EM/Resp |
| Pregnancy/Postpartum | Critical | missed ectopic/PE/eclampsia/sepsis | low threshold referral | OB/EM |
| Toxicology/Overdose | Critical | delayed poisoning care/self-harm | ED/poison escalation, no antidote dosing | EM/Tox |
| ECG Danger Recognition | Critical | wrong ECG reassurance | danger recognition only | EM/Cardiology |
| Pediatrics | High | subtle deterioration | danger signs + caregiver concern | Pediatrics/EM |
| Sepsis | High | delayed referral | toxic appearance/red flags | EM/ID |
| Trauma | High | missed internal injury | mechanism + ABCDE red flags | EM/Trauma |
| Mental Health | High | unsafe self-harm disposition | crisis/ED escalation | Psychiatry/EM |
| Back Pain | High | missed cauda equina/infection/malignancy | red-flag questions | EM/Neuro/Ortho |
| Dermatology | High | missed sepsis/SJS/anaphylaxis | systemic/mucosal red flags | EM/Derm |
