## Main Purpose

Emergency red flags, safe referral, and first-contact decision making for primary care doctors.

## What this path teaches

A GP/frontline clinician should know:

- when a presentation is unsafe to keep in clinic
- when reassurance is dangerous
- how to identify red flags
- how to communicate clearly with ED
- how to safety-net patients who do not currently need ED
- how to document uncertainty and escalation

## What this path does NOT teach

- ED resuscitation protocols
- drug dosing
- advanced procedural management
- specialty-specific inpatient care
- local triage rules
- definitive discharge decisions for high-risk presentations

## Golden Rule

GP Path = do not miss danger, do not falsely reassure, and refer clearly.
