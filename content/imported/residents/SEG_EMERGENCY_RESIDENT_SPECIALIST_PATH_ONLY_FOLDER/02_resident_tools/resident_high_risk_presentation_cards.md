# Resident Tools — High-Risk Presentation Cards

## Chest Pain

Do not miss:
- ACS/OMI
- dissection
- PE
- pneumothorax
- pericarditis/tamponade
- esophageal rupture

Disposition pause:
- ECG repeated if needed?
- symptoms and risk fit?
- serial data according to local protocol?
- alternative dangerous diagnoses considered?

## Dyspnea

Do not miss:
- respiratory failure
- PE
- pulmonary edema
- severe asthma/COPD
- pneumothorax
- anaphylaxis
- metabolic acidosis
- toxicologic hypoventilation

Disposition pause:
- work of breathing?
- mental status?
- oxygenation/ventilation?
- trajectory?

## Abdominal Pain

Do not miss:
- peritonitis/perforation
- mesenteric ischemia
- AAA
- ectopic pregnancy
- torsion
- bowel obstruction
- biliary sepsis
- pancreatitis
- DKA

Disposition pause:
- elderly/high-risk?
- pain out of proportion?
- serial abdominal exam?
- pregnancy considered?
- CT/US decision appropriate?

## Headache

Do not miss:
- SAH
- meningitis/encephalitis
- stroke/CVT
- mass/raised ICP
- temporal arteritis in appropriate age
- glaucoma
- pregnancy/postpartum emergency

Disposition pause:
- thunderclap?
- neuro deficits?
- fever/neck stiffness?
- pregnancy/postpartum?
- anticoagulant/trauma?

## Syncope

Do not miss:
- arrhythmia
- ACS
- PE
- hemorrhage
- aortic stenosis/structural disease
- ectopic pregnancy
- seizure mimic
- GI bleed

Disposition pause:
- exertional/supine?
- palpitations/chest pain?
- abnormal ECG?
- injury/anticoagulant?
- elderly/frail?
