# Supervising Interns & Medical Students

## Purpose

Help residents supervise safely in the ED.

## Why It Matters

Residents are often responsible for the learning and safety of junior team members.

## Supervision Principles

- define roles
- clarify what juniors may not do alone
- review unstable patients early
- verify high-risk histories/exams
- check abnormal vitals
- review ECGs and critical results
- review discharge decisions
- teach one point per case
- encourage early escalation
- use closed-loop communication

## Interns Need Help With

- prioritization
- when to call senior
- abnormal vitals
- handover
- disposition
- medication safety
- documentation
- reassessment

## Students Need Help With

- consent
- role identity
- history structure
- exam technique
- red flags
- presentation
- not acting beyond role

## Cognitive Traps

- assuming juniors will report everything
- not reviewing high-risk discharges
- too much teaching during instability
- unclear task delegation
- failing to close loops

## Exam Trap

Safe supervision is often the hidden answer in ED systems questions.
