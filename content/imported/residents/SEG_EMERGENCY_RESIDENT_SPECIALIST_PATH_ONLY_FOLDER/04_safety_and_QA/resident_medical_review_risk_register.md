# Resident / Specialist Path Medical Review Risk Register

| Module | Risk Level | Main Risk | Mitigation | Review Needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Shock | Critical | unsafe resuscitation implication | physiology + consult/disposition only | EM/Critical Care |
| Airway | Critical | airway procedure/drug risk | no steps/doses; high-level risk | EM/Anesthesia |
| Respiratory Failure | Critical | ventilator setting risk | conceptual only, no settings | EM/ICU |
| Chest Pain/ACS | Critical | missed ACS/overcalling protocol | risk thinking + local pathway | EM/Cardiology |
| ECG | Critical | dangerous misinterpretation | pattern recognition + senior review | EM/Cardiology |
| Sepsis | Critical | protocol timing/dosing claims | no fixed bundle; local protocol | EM/ID/ICU |
| Trauma | Critical | primary survey/OR decisions | concepts + local trauma pathway | EM/Trauma |
| Pediatrics | Critical | pediatric dosing/management risk | recognition + consult | Peds/EM |
| OB/GYN | Critical | pregnancy emergency risk | urgent consultation | OB/EM |
| Toxicology | Critical | antidote dose risk | no dosing; tox consult | EM/Tox |
| Disposition | Critical | unsafe discharge | risk framework + review | EM |
| Procedures | High | procedural harm | decision/consent only | EM/Procedural |
