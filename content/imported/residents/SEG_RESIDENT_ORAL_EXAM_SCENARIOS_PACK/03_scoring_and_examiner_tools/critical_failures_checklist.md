# Critical Failures Checklist

A candidate fails the scenario if they:

- ignores airway/breathing/circulation instability
- delays emergency response for detailed history
- fails to call for help in a crashing patient
- misses obvious shock
- misses STEMI/OMI or dangerous ECG pattern when clearly presented
- treats altered mental status as psychiatric without medical screen
- misses pregnancy possibility in reproductive-age abdominal pain/syncope
- misses pediatric toxicity/lethargy/respiratory distress
- misses self-harm risk after intentional overdose
- discharges a high-risk patient without reassessment/risk explanation
- fails to reassess after deterioration
- ignores abnormal vital signs
- gives medication doses from memory in an unsafe/general context
- claims a local protocol without knowing local policy
