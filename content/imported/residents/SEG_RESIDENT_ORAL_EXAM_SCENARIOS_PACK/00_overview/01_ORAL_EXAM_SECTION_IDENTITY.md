# Oral Exam Section Identity

## Section Name Options

Preferred:
**Resident Oral Exam Scenarios**

Alternatives:
- Emergency Oral Exam Simulation
- Board Oral Cases
- Case-Based Resident Review
- Oral Board Practice

## Target Users

- Emergency medicine residents
- Emergency specialists
- Board candidates
- Senior interns preparing for advanced ED reasoning
- Trainers creating oral simulation sessions

## What It Teaches

- structured first response
- prioritization under uncertainty
- safe resuscitation language
- high-risk differential diagnosis
- asking for the right data at the right time
- recognizing deterioration
- consultant and disposition communication
- avoiding oral-exam critical failures

## What It Does NOT Teach

- official exam content
- medication doses
- local protocols
- procedural manuals
- exact answer scripts to memorize
- independent clinical management outside supervision/local guidelines

## Arabic Pearl

الامتحان الشفوي في الطوارئ لا يختبر التشخيص فقط؛ يختبر كيف تفكر وتتصرف بأمان تحت الضغط.
