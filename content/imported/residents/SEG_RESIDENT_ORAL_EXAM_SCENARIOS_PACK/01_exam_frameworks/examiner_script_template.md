# Examiner Script Template

## Opening

“You are the emergency resident. A patient arrives with [chief complaint]. Tell me your first steps.”

## Examiner should reveal only what candidate asks for

Possible data categories:
- general appearance
- vital signs
- focused history
- focused exam
- ECG
- labs
- imaging
- POCUS
- response to interventions under local protocol
- consultant availability
- case progression

## Scoring Domains

1. First priorities and ABCDE
2. Recognition of life threats
3. Differential diagnosis
4. Data request strategy
5. Reassessment after new data
6. Consultation and disposition
7. Communication clarity
8. Avoidance of critical failures

## Examiner Prompt Examples

- “What are you most worried about?”
- “What data do you want now?”
- “The patient deteriorates. What do you do?”
- “Who do you call?”
- “Where should this patient go?”
- “What would be unsafe here?”
- “What is the common trap?”
