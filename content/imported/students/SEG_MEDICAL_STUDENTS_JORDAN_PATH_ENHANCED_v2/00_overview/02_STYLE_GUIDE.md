# Medical Students Path Style Guide

## Language

Main UI/content:
English

Arabic:
Short pearls only.

## Tone

- Student-friendly
- Structured
- Clinical but safe
- OSCE-aware
- Jordan-context aware
- No overconfidence

## Page structure per module

Each module should include:

1. Learning goal
2. Why it matters in Jordanian clinical training
3. What the student should know
4. What the student should do under supervision
5. What the student must not do alone
6. OSCE / exam trap
7. Clinical red flags when relevant
8. Arabic pearl

## Preferred wording

Use:
- observe
- recognize
- present
- report to supervisor
- ask for senior review
- under supervision
- red flags
- OSCE structure
- clerkship readiness

Avoid:
- treat with
- administer
- discharge
- manage independently
- definitive protocol
- dose/dosage
