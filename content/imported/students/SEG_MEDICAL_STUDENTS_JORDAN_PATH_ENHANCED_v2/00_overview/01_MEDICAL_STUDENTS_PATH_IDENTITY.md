# Medical Students Path — Identity

## Path Name

Medical Students Path  
طلاب الطب

## Main Purpose

Help Jordanian medical students move from classroom knowledge to safe clinical recognition, bedside structure, OSCE performance, and emergency red-flag thinking.

## Target users

- Preclinical medical students: years 1–3
- Clinical medical students: years 4–6
- Students preparing for clinical rotations
- Students rotating in hospitals, Ministry of Health sites, university hospitals, or primary-care/community settings
- Students preparing for internship/JMC-style thinking later

## What this path teaches

A medical student should learn:

- how to approach a patient safely
- how to structure history and exam
- how to recognize emergency red flags
- how to present cases clearly
- how to connect basic sciences with clinical patterns
- how to behave professionally in Jordanian clinical environments
- how to prepare for OSCEs and clerkships
- how to observe ED cases without acting beyond role
- how to transition safely toward internship

## What this path does NOT teach

- independent treatment protocols
- medication dosing
- procedural instructions
- replacing senior supervision
- replacing official university curricula
- replacing Ministry of Health or hospital policies

## Golden Rule

Medical Student Path = understand, observe, recognize danger, communicate clearly, and never act beyond supervision.

## Arabic Pearl
