# Jordan Public Medical Universities — SEG Alignment Cards

## Purpose

This file supports the Medical Students Jordan Path by mapping SEG content to shared learning needs across Jordanian public medical schools. It is not an official curriculum map and must not be displayed as university endorsement.

## Official Source Anchors to Keep in Source Map

- Jordan Ministry of Health homepage and Health Sector Guide.
- University of Jordan School of Medicine / MD study plan pages.
- Jordan University of Science and Technology Faculty of Medicine study plan and syllabuses.
- Mutah University Faculty of Medicine study plans.
- Hashemite University Doctor of Medicine page and study plan.
- Yarmouk University Faculty of Medicine MD study plan page.
- Al-Balqa Applied University Faculty of Medicine M.D study plan.

## Shared Competency Areas

1. Preclinical-to-clinical transition.
2. History and examination.
3. Professionalism, consent, privacy, and patient safety.
4. Clinical rotations and clerkship behavior.
5. OSCE readiness.
6. Emergency red-flag recognition.
7. Primary-care and hospital workflow awareness.
8. Research/EBM basics.
9. Internship readiness.

## Display Rule

Use wording like:

“Designed for Jordanian medical students and informed by public university and Ministry of Health context.”

Avoid wording like:

“Official curriculum for Jordanian medical schools.”
