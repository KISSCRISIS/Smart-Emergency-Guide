# Common ED Presentations for Students

## Learning Goal

Help students approach common ED complaints safely.

## Presentations to Learn

- chest pain
- shortness of breath
- abdominal pain
- fever/sepsis concern
- altered mental status
- trauma
- seizure
- syncope
- headache
- allergic reaction/anaphylaxis concern
- overdose/poisoning
- pediatric fever/dehydration
- pregnancy-related emergency symptoms

## For Each Presentation Ask

- Is the patient stable?
- What are the red flags?
- What dangerous diagnoses should not be missed?
- What history questions matter most?
- What exam findings matter most?
- What should be reported now?
- How would I present this case?

## Student ED Observation Task

For each ED case, write:

- presenting complaint
- first impression
- red flags present/absent
- top 3 differentials
- key tests ordered by team
- what changed management
- what you learned

## Common Student Trap

Memorizing diagnosis lists without learning what makes the patient dangerous.
