# Pediatrics for Medical Students

## Learning Goal

Teach students pediatric danger recognition and safe communication.

## Student Focus

- age matters
- appearance matters
- feeding and hydration
- breathing effort
- caregiver concern
- developmental baseline
- fever risk by age
- safeguarding awareness

## Pediatric Red Flags

Report urgently:
- lethargy
- poor feeding in infant
- respiratory distress
- cyanosis
- seizure
- non-blanching rash
- dehydration concern
- reduced urine
- persistent vomiting
- altered behavior
- safeguarding concern
- caregiver says child is not normal

## Student Communication

Ask caregiver:
- what changed?
- feeding?
- urine?
- breathing?
- fever?
- rash?
- vomiting/diarrhea?
- activity compared to baseline?

## Common Student Trap

Using adult patterns to judge children.
