# Infection Prevention & PPE

## Learning Goal

Teach students safe infection-control behavior in clinical training.

## Core Behaviors

- hand hygiene
- appropriate PPE
- do not attend clinical areas when unwell according to policy
- respect isolation signs
- clean equipment as required
- avoid touching face/phone/gloves carelessly
- sharps safety
- report needle-stick exposure immediately
- follow local hospital/MOH policy

## Student Must Know

- standard precautions
- contact/droplet/airborne concept
- hand hygiene moments
- safe disposal
- patient privacy while using PPE
- infection risk to self and others

## Common Student Trap

Wearing gloves but forgetting hand hygiene.
