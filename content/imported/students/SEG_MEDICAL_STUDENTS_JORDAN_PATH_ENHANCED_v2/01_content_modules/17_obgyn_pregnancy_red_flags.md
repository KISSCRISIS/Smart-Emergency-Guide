# OB/GYN & Pregnancy Red Flags for Students

## Learning Goal

Teach students pregnancy and gynecology red flags.

## Must-Recognize Presentations

- abdominal/pelvic pain with possible pregnancy
- vaginal bleeding in pregnancy
- syncope/collapse in pregnancy
- severe headache/visual symptoms in pregnancy/postpartum
- seizure in pregnancy/postpartum
- chest pain/dyspnea postpartum
- fever/toxic appearance postpartum
- severe pelvic pain
- heavy bleeding
- sexual assault/safeguarding concern

## Student Role

- ask pregnancy possibility respectfully
- know LMP/pregnancy status matters
- report red flags immediately
- maintain privacy and dignity
- request chaperone according to rules
- do not independently examine intimate areas

## Common Student Trap

Forgetting pregnancy possibility in abdominal pain.
