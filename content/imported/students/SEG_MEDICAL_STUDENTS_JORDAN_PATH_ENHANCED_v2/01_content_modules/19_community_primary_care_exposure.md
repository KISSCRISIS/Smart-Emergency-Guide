# Community & Primary Care Exposure

## Learning Goal

Help students learn from Ministry of Health and primary-care settings.

## What To Observe

- common complaints
- chronic disease follow-up
- vaccination/prevention
- maternal-child health
- public health education
- referral decisions
- safety-netting
- documentation
- social determinants of health
- continuity of care

## Student Tasks

For each clinic day, write:

- 3 common complaints
- 2 red flags discussed
- 1 referral decision
- 1 safety-net phrase
- 1 public health lesson
- 1 communication challenge

## Primary Care Red Flags

Students should know when primary care is not enough:
- chest pain red flags
- dyspnea red flags
- neurologic deficit
- pregnancy danger
- pediatric toxic appearance
- suspected sepsis
- severe dehydration
- self-harm/overdose

## Common Student Trap

Thinking primary care is less clinical than hospital medicine.
