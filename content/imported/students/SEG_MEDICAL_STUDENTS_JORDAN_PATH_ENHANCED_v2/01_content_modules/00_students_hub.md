# Medical Students Path Hub

**Title:** Medical Students Path  
**Subtitle:** From basic science to safe bedside reasoning in Jordanian clinical training  
**Arabic subtitle:** مسار طلاب الطب: من العلوم الأساسية إلى التفكير السريري الآمن

**Disclaimer:**  
General educational framework for medical students. This pathway does not replace official university curricula, Ministry of Health policies, hospital rules, clinical supervision, or senior medical judgment.

## Path Purpose

This pathway helps medical students build a safe structure for clinical learning:

- connect preclinical systems to real patient presentations
- recognize dangerous symptoms early
- prepare for OSCEs and clinical rotations
- learn ED and primary-care observation safely
- present cases clearly
- understand when to call a supervisor

## Student Stage Cards

### Preclinical Student

Focus:
- systems thinking
- anatomy/physiology/pathology connection
- medical terminology
- basic clinical orientation
- ethics and professionalism
- early red-flag awareness

### Early Clinical Student

Focus:
- history taking
- physical examination
- differential diagnosis
- OSCE structure
- common clinical presentations
- documentation basics
- presentation skills

### Senior Clinical Student

Focus:
- clerkship survival
- ED observation
- referral and handover
- patient safety
- red flags
- internship readiness
- JMC-style reasoning foundation
