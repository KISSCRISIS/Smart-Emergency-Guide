# Mental Health & Self-Harm Safety for Students

## Learning Goal

Teach students to recognize urgent mental health safety concerns.

## Red Flags

Report immediately if:
- suicidal thoughts with plan/intent
- recent self-harm
- overdose
- psychosis with unsafe behavior
- severe agitation
- risk to others
- unsafe home situation
- intoxication plus self-harm risk
- safeguarding concern
- inability to care for self

## Student Role

Students may:
- listen respectfully
- ask direct safety questions under supervision
- report concerns immediately
- avoid judgment
- protect privacy
- ensure patient is not left unsafe according to team instructions

Students must not:
- promise secrecy
- allow unsafe patient to leave without telling supervisor
- manage severe agitation alone
- assume psychiatric cause before medical causes are considered

## Common Student Trap

Avoiding suicide questions because they feel uncomfortable.
