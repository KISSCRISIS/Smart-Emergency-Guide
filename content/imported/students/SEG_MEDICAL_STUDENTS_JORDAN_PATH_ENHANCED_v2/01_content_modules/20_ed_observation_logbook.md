# ED Observation Logbook

## Learning Goal

Give students a safe structure to learn during ED exposure.

## Case Log Template

For each observed ED case:

- Date:
- Setting:
- Presenting complaint:
- First impression:
- Initial stability:
- Red flags:
- Top differentials:
- Key history questions:
- Key exam findings:
- Tests ordered by team:
- What changed management:
- Handover point:
- Learning pearl:
- What I would review tonight:

## High-Yield ED Cases to Observe

- chest pain
- dyspnea
- sepsis concern
- trauma
- seizure
- altered mental status
- abdominal pain
- pediatric fever
- overdose
- syncope
- allergic reaction

## Reflection Questions

- What made the case urgent?
- What was the dangerous diagnosis?
- How did the team communicate?
- What was the senior decision point?
- What did I not understand?

## Common Student Trap

Watching passively without extracting a learning point.
