# OSCE Survival for Medical Students

## Learning Goal

Teach students a safe, repeatable OSCE structure.

## Universal OSCE Opening

- wash hands
- introduce yourself
- confirm patient identity if appropriate
- explain task
- ask consent
- ensure privacy
- ask about pain
- position patient
- expose appropriately
- thank patient
- summarize findings
- mention red flags/escalation

## History OSCE

Structure:
- presenting complaint
- HPI
- associated symptoms
- red flags
- PMH
- medications
- allergies
- family/social history
- systems review
- summary

## Examination OSCE

Structure:
- inspection
- focused exam
- relevant special tests
- function
- finish with what you would do next

## Emergency OSCE

Start with:
- ABCDE
- call for help
- monitor/vitals
- focused danger recognition
- communicate clearly

## Common OSCE Traps

- forgetting consent
- no hand hygiene
- no pain question
- no red flags
- no summary
- over-talking and under-structuring
- pretending to do actions not allowed

## Arabic Pearl

الـ OSCE ليس حفظ خطوات فقط؛ هو إظهار أنك آمن ومنظم وتحترم المريض.
