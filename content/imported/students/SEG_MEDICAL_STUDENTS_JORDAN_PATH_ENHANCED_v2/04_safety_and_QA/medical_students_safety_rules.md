# Medical Students Path Safety Rules

## Core Rule

Medical Students Path is for education, observation, supervised skills, and red-flag recognition.

It is not a treatment pathway.

## Must include

- disclaimer
- supervised role
- red flags
- when to tell supervisor
- student limitations
- OSCE/learning framing
- Arabic pearls only

## Must avoid

- medication doses
- drug administration instructions
- independent treatment plans
- local hospital protocols
- fixed triage times
- procedures as instructions
- independent discharge decisions
- copied text/questions from sources
- claiming official MOH/university curriculum

## Scan terms

Search for:
- mg
- mcg
- mL
- units/kg
- dose
- dosage
- bolus
- infusion
- drip
- administer
- give
- treat with
- protocol
- discharge
- independent decision
- official curriculum

Context review is required:
Some terms may appear in warnings. Read the context before failing.
