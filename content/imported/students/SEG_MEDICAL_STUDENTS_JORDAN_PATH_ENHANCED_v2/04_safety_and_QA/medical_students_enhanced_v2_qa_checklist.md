# Medical Students Jordan Path Enhanced v2 — QA Checklist

## Must Pass

- No official Ministry of Health curriculum claim.
- No official university endorsement claim.
- No copied university questions, syllabi, tables, or PDF wording.
- No medication doses.
- No procedure instructions that imply independent student action.
- Every high-risk module includes supervised boundaries.
- Red flags tell students to escalate, not treat independently.
- Arabic content remains short, practical, and patient-facing.
- Routes keep Students separate from Intern/JMC, GP, EMS, and Resident paths.

## Content QA

- Jordan Public Medical Schools Alignment is framed as shared competency support.
- MOH Workflow module explains observation and referral awareness without operational authority.
- Clerkship maps are practical and student-safe.
- OSCE bank is original SEG content.
- Logbook removes patient identifiers.
- Student-to-Intern bridge links forward to Intern/JMC path only after boundaries are clear.

## Implementation QA

- Build `/students` hub first.
- Do not show every subpage in the global homepage.
- Use badges instead of fake completion claims.
- Mark high-risk clinical pages as supervised.
- Link official references from source map/admin notes, not as copied body content.
