# Medical Students Path Risk Register

| Module | Risk Level | Main Risk | Mitigation | Review Needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Vitals/ABCDE | High | student acting beyond role | supervised recognition only | EM/Clinical skills |
| Red Flags | High | false reassurance | report to supervisor | EM |
| ED Presentations | High | perceived ED management instructions | observation and recognition only | EM |
| Labs/ECG/Imaging | High | overconfident interpretation | context + report uncertainty | EM/Radiology/Cardiology |
| Medication Safety | High | prescribing/dosing risk | no doses; no orders | Pharmacology/Clinical |
| Pediatrics | High | subtle child deterioration | red flags + senior escalation | Pediatrics |
| OB/GYN | High | pregnancy danger | urgent report/supervision | OB/EM |
| Mental Health | High | unsafe self-harm handling | immediate supervisor/crisis escalation | Psychiatry |
| Internship Readiness | High | overconfidence | limitations and escalation | Clinical supervisor |
| Professionalism | Moderate | privacy/consent errors | consent and confidentiality rules | Faculty/Clinical |
