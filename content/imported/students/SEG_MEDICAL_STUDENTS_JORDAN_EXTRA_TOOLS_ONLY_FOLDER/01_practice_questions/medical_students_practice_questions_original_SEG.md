# Medical Students Jordan Path — Original Practice Questions

**Label:** Practice question — original SEG educational question, not an official university/JMC/Rosh/Rosen/Tintinalli question.

**Purpose:** Student-level clinical reasoning, professionalism, OSCE readiness, red flags, and supervised role awareness.

---

## Question 1 — Professionalism

**Practice question — original SEG educational question, not an official exam question.**

A student enters a patient room in a teaching hospital. What is the safest first step?

A. Start the examination immediately
B. Introduce self as a medical student and ask permission
C. Ask personal questions before explaining role
D. Begin presenting the patient to the team

**Answer:** B

**Explanation:** Students must identify themselves clearly and obtain consent before history or examination.
