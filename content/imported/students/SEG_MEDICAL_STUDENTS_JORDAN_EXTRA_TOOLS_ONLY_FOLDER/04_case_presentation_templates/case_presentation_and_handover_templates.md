# Medical Students Case Presentation Templates

## One-Minute Case Presentation

This is a [age]-year-old [sex] presenting with [chief complaint] for [duration].

Key associated symptoms include [positives]. Important negatives/red flags include [negatives].

Relevant background includes [PMH], [medications], [allergies], and [social/family context if relevant].

On examination, [general appearance/vitals/key findings].

My top differentials are:
1.
2.
3.

I am most concerned about [danger diagnosis/red flag] because [reason].

I would like senior review/advice regarding [question].

## ISBAR Student Handover

**I — Identify**  
I am [name], medical student with [team]. I am presenting [patient age/sex/location].

**S — Situation**  
The current issue is [main problem]. I am concerned because [red flag].

**B — Background**  
Relevant history includes [PMH/medications/allergies/context].

**A — Assessment**  
Current findings are [appearance/vitals/exam/key results]. My concern is [differential].

**R — Recommendation / Request**  
Could you please review because [reason]? Pending items are [tests/reassessment].

---

## OSCE Summary Ending

To summarize, this patient has [main complaint] with [key features]. The main red flags I looked for were [red flags]. My initial differentials include [differentials]. I would discuss this with my senior and proceed according to local clinical guidance.

---

## Red-Flag Reporting Phrase

“Doctor, I am concerned about this patient because they have [red flag]. Their current status is [appearance/vitals]. I think they need urgent review.”

---

## Student Uncertainty Phrase

“I am not sure of the diagnosis, but I am concerned because [red flag/context]. I would like to ask for senior review.”
