# EMS / Paramedics Path Risk Register

| Module | Risk Level | Main Risk | Mitigation | Review Needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Airway/Breathing | Critical | unsafe airway/procedure implication | recognition only, local protocol disclaimer | EMS/EM |
| Shock/Bleeding | Critical | protocol-like treatment advice | red flags and communication only | EMS/EM/Trauma |
| Chest Pain | Critical | STEMI/ACS misinterpretation | handover and escalation only | EM/Cardiology |
| Dyspnea | Critical | respiratory failure missed | danger signs, no procedure steps | EM/Resp |
| Stroke | Critical | time-sensitive pathway claims | timeline/handover only | EM/Neuro |
| Trauma | Critical | transport/triage protocol claims | mechanism/red flags only | EM/Trauma |
| Anaphylaxis | Critical | medication dose risk | no dosing; local protocol | EM/EMS |
| Pregnancy | Critical | obstetric emergency | recognition and ED notification | OB/EM |
| Toxicology | Critical | antidote/decontamination risk | recognition only, no doses | EM/Tox |
| ECG Danger | Critical | wrong interpretation | danger recognition only | EM/Cardiology |
| Mass Casualty | High | local triage protocol claims | concept only, local command | EMS/Disaster |
