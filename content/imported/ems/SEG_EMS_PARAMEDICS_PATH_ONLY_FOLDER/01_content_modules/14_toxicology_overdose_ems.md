# Toxicology / Overdose Recognition

## Purpose

Help EMS recognize poisoning and overdose danger.

## Red Flags

- reduced consciousness
- respiratory depression
- seizure
- severe agitation
- abnormal vitals with abnormal behavior
- chest pain after stimulant use
- intentional overdose
- pediatric ingestion
- unknown substance
- multiple co-ingestions
- long-acting/sustained-release concern
- carbon monoxide concern
- chemical exposure
- pregnancy
- recurrent symptoms after improvement

## EMS Questions

- what substance
- when
- how much
- co-ingestions
- intent/self-harm
- containers at scene
- alcohol/substances
- occupational/environmental exposure
- multiple patients affected

## What To Communicate

- suspected agent
- time and amount estimate
- mental status
- airway/breathing/circulation
- ECG concern if available
- glucose if available
- self-harm risk
- scene clues

## Common Prehospital Trap

Believing the first history completely.
