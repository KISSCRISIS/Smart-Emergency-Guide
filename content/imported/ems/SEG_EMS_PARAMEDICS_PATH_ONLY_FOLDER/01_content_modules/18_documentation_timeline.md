# EMS Documentation & Timeline

## Purpose

Help EMS document what matters clinically and legally.

## Document

- dispatch time
- arrival time
- patient contact time
- departure time
- hospital arrival time
- chief complaint/mechanism
- first impression
- ABCDE concerns
- vital trends if available
- mental status trend
- interventions under protocol
- response to interventions
- patient statements
- bystander information
- medication/allergy information if known
- handover recipient
- refusal/consent issues according to local policy
- safety concerns

## Timeline Matters

For:
- stroke
- chest pain
- trauma
- seizure
- overdose
- pregnancy bleeding
- collapse/syncope
- altered mental status

## Common Prehospital Trap

Documenting only final status and losing the timeline.
