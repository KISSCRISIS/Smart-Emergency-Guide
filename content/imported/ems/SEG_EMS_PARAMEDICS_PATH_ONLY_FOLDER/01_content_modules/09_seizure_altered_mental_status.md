# Seizure & Altered Mental Status

## Purpose

Help EMS recognize dangerous causes of seizure and altered mental status.

## Red Flags

- ongoing seizure or recurrent seizures
- prolonged postictal state
- trauma/head injury
- pregnancy/postpartum
- fever/meningitis concern
- hypoglycemia/metabolic concern
- overdose/toxicology concern
- stroke signs
- first seizure
- child or elderly patient
- anticoagulant use
- violent agitation with abnormal physiology

## What To Notice

- airway/breathing risk
- injury
- duration/timing
- witness history
- baseline mental status
- glucose if available and within scope
- medications/substances
- pregnancy/postpartum
- fever/rash/neck stiffness

## What To Communicate

- seizure duration/count
- recovery status
- airway/breathing concerns
- trauma
- glucose if available
- suspected cause
- trend during transport

## Common Prehospital Trap

Assuming altered behavior is psychiatric before checking medical causes.
