# Anaphylaxis & Severe Allergy Recognition

## Purpose

Help EMS recognize systemic allergic emergency.

## Red Flags

- airway swelling
- voice change
- stridor
- wheeze/shortness of breath
- hypotension/shock appearance
- syncope
- vomiting with allergic features
- widespread rash plus breathing/circulation symptoms
- known severe allergy exposure

## EMS Questions

- exposure trigger
- timing
- prior anaphylaxis
- medication/food/insect exposure
- breathing symptoms
- swelling
- GI symptoms
- collapse/syncope

## What To Communicate

- suspected trigger
- airway/breathing/circulation involvement
- symptom progression
- treatment given under local protocol
- response/deterioration
- estimated arrival

## Common Prehospital Trap

Waiting for rash to appear before considering anaphylaxis.
