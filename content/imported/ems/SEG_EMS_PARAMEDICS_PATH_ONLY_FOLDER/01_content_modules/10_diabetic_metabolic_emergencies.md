# Diabetic / Metabolic Emergencies

## Purpose

Help EMS identify metabolic danger in the field.

## Red Flags

- altered mental status
- seizure
- sweating/weakness with glucose concern
- vomiting with diabetes
- abdominal pain with diabetes
- rapid/deep breathing
- dehydration signs
- recurrent hypoglycemia concern
- severe infection in diabetic patient
- renal failure/electrolyte concern

## EMS Questions

- diabetes history
- medications if known
- last meal
- recent illness/vomiting
- glucose reading if available
- seizure or collapse
- infection symptoms

## What To Communicate

- mental status
- glucose if available
- diabetes medications if known
- vomiting/dehydration
- infection concern
- response to protocol-based care if performed

## Common Prehospital Trap

Treating the number without assessing the whole patient.
