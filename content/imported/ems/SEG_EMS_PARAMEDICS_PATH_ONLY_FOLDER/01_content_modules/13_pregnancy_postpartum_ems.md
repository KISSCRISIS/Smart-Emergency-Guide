# Pregnancy & Postpartum Emergencies

## Purpose

Help EMS recognize pregnancy/postpartum red flags.

## Red Flags

- abdominal/pelvic pain with pregnancy possibility
- vaginal bleeding with pregnancy
- syncope/collapse
- severe headache/visual symptoms
- seizure
- chest pain or dyspnea
- heavy postpartum bleeding
- fever/toxic appearance postpartum
- trauma in pregnancy
- reduced fetal movement in later pregnancy when reported

## EMS Questions

- pregnancy possibility
- gestational age if known
- postpartum timing
- bleeding/pain
- headache/visual symptoms
- seizure/syncope
- chest pain/dyspnea
- trauma
- fetal movement if relevant

## What To Communicate

- pregnancy/postpartum status
- gestational age/timing
- bleeding/pain
- vital stability if available
- neurologic symptoms
- trauma
- estimated arrival

## Common Prehospital Trap

Forgetting pregnancy possibility in abdominal pain or syncope.
