# Safety Audit Before Public Release

## Must Verify

- Antidote indications and contraindications.
- Local formulary availability.
- Poison center / toxicologist escalation points.
- Dialysis indications for lithium, salicylate, methanol, ethylene glycol, valproate, theophylline, and other dialyzable toxins.
- Activated charcoal, whole bowel irrigation, and decontamination indications.
- Pediatric poison-specific exceptions.
- Pregnancy-specific toxicology considerations.
- Local disposition and observation policies.

## Do Not Add Without Review

- Fixed medication doses.
- Infusion rates.
- Rigid observation times.
- Dialysis thresholds.
- “Always/never” treatment claims where local toxicology guidance varies.

## High-Risk Pages for Audit Priority

1. Acetaminophen Toxicity
2. Salicylate Toxicity
3. Toxic Alcohols
4. Lithium Toxicity
5. TCA / Sodium-Channel Blocker Toxicity
6. Digoxin / Cardiac Glycoside Toxicity
7. Beta-Blocker and Calcium-Channel Blocker shock layer, if added later
8. Cyanide / Smoke Inhalation
9. Organophosphate Poisoning
10. Hydrofluoric Acid Exposure
