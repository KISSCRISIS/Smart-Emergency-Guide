# References and Medical Audit Sources

- **Tintinalli Board Review 2023:** Tintinalli’s Emergency Medicine Examination and Board Review, 2023, Chapter 13: Toxicologic Disorders. Used as the primary board-style topic map and exam-trap source; content below is rewritten for SEG and not copied.
- **Poison Centers:** America’s Poison Centers / Poison Help: https://www.poisoncenters.org and https://www.poison.org
- **ACMT/AACT:** American College of Medical Toxicology and American Academy of Clinical Toxicology position statements: https://www.acmt.net and https://www.clintox.org/resources/position-statements
- **EXTRIP:** EXTRIP Workgroup recommendations for extracorporeal treatment in poisoning: https://www.extrip-workgroup.org
- **Activated Charcoal:** AACT/EAPCCT position paper on single-dose activated charcoal and position statement on multi-dose activated charcoal.
- **Goldfrank:** Goldfrank’s Toxicologic Emergencies, current edition, for toxicology mechanisms and antidote details during final medical audit.
- **ATSDR/CDC:** ATSDR/CDC Medical Management Guidelines for chemical exposures where applicable: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/TSP/MMG/MMGHome.aspx


## Source-Use Boundary

Tintinalli Board Review is used as a topic and board-trap map, not as copied content. Final public site release should verify each antidote and disposition item against current toxicology guidance, Poison Center recommendations, EXTRIP where applicable, and local hospital protocols.
