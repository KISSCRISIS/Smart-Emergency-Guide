# Physostigmine

> **SEG Safety Layer**
> This page is for emergency medicine learning and board-style pattern recognition. It is not a local treatment protocol. For real patients, stabilize ABCs, call your local poison center/toxicologist early, and follow local guidelines. Doses, infusion rates, dialysis thresholds, and disposition times must be verified locally before use.


## Indication

Selected pure anticholinergic delirium after ECG review and toxicology consultation.

## Mechanism

Reversible acetylcholinesterase inhibitor increasing acetylcholine at synapses.

## When to Think of It

Dry delirious patient with anticholinergic toxidrome and no concerning sodium-channel blockade.

## Key Monitoring

Monitor ECG, seizures, bradycardia, and recurrence.

## Dangerous Trap

Dangerous if TCA/sodium-channel blocker toxicity is possible.
