# Vitamin K

> **SEG Safety Layer**
> This page is for emergency medicine learning and board-style pattern recognition. It is not a local treatment protocol. For real patients, stabilize ABCs, call your local poison center/toxicologist early, and follow local guidelines. Doses, infusion rates, dialysis thresholds, and disposition times must be verified locally before use.


## Indication

Clinically significant warfarin/superwarfarin anticoagulation with elevated INR/bleeding risk per protocol.

## Mechanism

Restores vitamin K-dependent clotting factor synthesis.

## When to Think of It

Rodenticide exposure, delayed INR elevation, bleeding/bruising.

## Key Monitoring

Monitor PT/INR trend and duration; superwarfarins can be prolonged.

## Dangerous Trap

Not every exploratory pediatric taste needs immediate vitamin K.
