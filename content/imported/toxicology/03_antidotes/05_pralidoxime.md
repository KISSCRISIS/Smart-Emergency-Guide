# Pralidoxime

> **SEG Safety Layer**
> This page is for emergency medicine learning and board-style pattern recognition. It is not a local treatment protocol. For real patients, stabilize ABCs, call your local poison center/toxicologist early, and follow local guidelines. Doses, infusion rates, dialysis thresholds, and disposition times must be verified locally before use.


## Indication

Organophosphate poisoning with nicotinic effects or severe cholinergic crisis.

## Mechanism

Reactivates acetylcholinesterase before aging, improving nicotinic weakness.

## When to Think of It

Pesticide/nerve-agent exposure with secretions plus weakness/fasciculations.

## Key Monitoring

Monitor muscle strength, ventilation, secretions, and response with atropine.

## Dangerous Trap

Delay reduces effectiveness after enzyme aging.
