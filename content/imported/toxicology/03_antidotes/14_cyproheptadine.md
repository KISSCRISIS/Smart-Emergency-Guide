# Cyproheptadine

> **SEG Safety Layer**
> This page is for emergency medicine learning and board-style pattern recognition. It is not a local treatment protocol. For real patients, stabilize ABCs, call your local poison center/toxicologist early, and follow local guidelines. Doses, infusion rates, dialysis thresholds, and disposition times must be verified locally before use.


## Indication

Adjunct for serotonin syndrome when supportive care and sedation/cooling are insufficient, per expert guidance.

## Mechanism

Serotonin receptor antagonism.

## When to Think of It

Clonus, hyperreflexia, agitation, hyperthermia after serotonergic exposure.

## Key Monitoring

Monitor mental status, temperature, clonus/rigidity, and airway protection.

## Dangerous Trap

It does not replace aggressive cooling/sedation in severe hyperthermia.
