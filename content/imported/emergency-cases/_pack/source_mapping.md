# Source Mapping

## Tintinalli mapping approach
Tintinalli’s board-review PDF was used to choose high-yield emergency presentations and board-style traps across resuscitation, cardiovascular, respiratory, abdominal, pediatrics, toxicology, environmental injuries, OB/GYN, trauma, and POCUS domains.

## Cross-package reuse
This package intentionally cross-links conceptually to prior SEG baselines:
- ECG Essentials: arrhythmia, ischemia, hyperkalemia, Brugada, Wellens, VF/VT.
- Toxicology Essentials: opioid, TCA, salicylate, acetaminophen, organophosphate, toxic alcohols, CO/cyanide, serotonin syndrome, lithium.
- Drug Essentials: drug names may appear conceptually, but no final numeric dosing is published here.

## Copyright safety
No Tintinalli questions, answer explanations, tables, or figures are reproduced. All cases are original SEG educational cases.
