# Medical Safety & Source Verification Rule

Smart Emergency Guide is an educational and exam-preparation platform. It must not be treated as a clinical order set.

Before final release, every high-risk clinical item must pass a verification review:

1. Medication doses and concentrations
2. Pediatric doses
3. RSI medications
4. Vasopressors/inotropes and infusion rates
5. Antidote doses and toxicology protocols
6. Sodium bicarbonate and electrolyte emergency protocols
7. Ventilator initial settings and mode-specific cautions
8. Contraindications and dangerous complications
9. Local availability/formulary differences
10. Disposition and escalation recommendations

Required wording inside relevant pages:

> Verify with local hospital protocol / pharmacy / toxicology or poison center / ICU or senior clinician before clinical use.

Arabic support wording:

> أي جرعة أو إعدادات منفسة أو ترياق أو قرار علاجي حساس يجب مراجعته مع بروتوكول المستشفى أو الطبيب المسؤول قبل التطبيق السريري.

This rule is especially important for Drug Handbook, Toxicology & Antidotes, Critical Care, Intubation & Ventilator, ECG emergency actions, and Algorithms.
